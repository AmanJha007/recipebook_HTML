Chole Puri

Chole Puri is a beloved North Indian dish that combines spicy chickpea curry (chole) with deep-fried bread (puri).
This flavorful and satisfying meal.A rich, spicy, and tangy chickpea curry served alongside hot, puffy, deep-fried bread.
It is the perfect weekend breakfast to keep you full all day.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup chickpeas (soaked overnight)
  • 2 large onions (finely chopped)
  • 2 large tomatoes (pureed)
  • 2 green chilies (slit)
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves (chopped, for garnish)
  • 2 cups whole wheat flour (for puris)
  • Water (as needed for dough)
  • Oil (for deep frying)

Steps for cooking

  1. Cook the soaked chickpeas in a pressure cooker with enough water until they are soft. Set aside.
  2. In a large pan, heat some oil and add cumin seeds. Once they splutter, add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and green chilies, and cook for a minute until fragrant.
  4. Pour in the tomato puree and cook until the oil starts to separate from the masala.
  5. Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, garam masala, and salt. Mix well.
  6. Add the cooked chickpeas along with some of the cooking water to achieve the desired consistency. Simmer for 10-15 minutes.
  7. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves before serving.
  8. For the puris, mix whole wheat flour with water to form a stiff dough. Roll out small portions into circles and deep fry in hot oil until they puff up and turn golden brown.
  9. Serve the hot chole with crispy puris and enjoy!
